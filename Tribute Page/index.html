CHATRAPATI SHIVAJI MAHARAJ


Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was one of the bravest, most progressive and sensible rulers of India. The founder of the Maratha Kingdom.

About the Legend

Shivaji Maharaj was born in the hill-fort of Shivneri, near the city of Junnar in Pune district of Maharastra on 19 February 1630

☛ Shivaji Maharaj was crowned as Chhatrapati of Raigad, on June 6th, 1674

☛ From a small contingent of 2,000 soldiers 000 soldiers. inherited from his father, he created a force of 1,00,000 soldiers

☛ Pioneered Guerrilla Warfare Methods, referred to as Shiva Sutra

☛ Promoted use of Marathi & Sanskrit, rather than Persian in administration

☛ He is considered as FATHER OF INDIAN NAVY

☛ He built a strong naval presence across the coast of Konkan and Goa to protect sea trade.

☛ Shivaji Maharaj built ships in towns such as Kalyan, Bhivandi, & Goa for building fighting navy as well as trade.
